Our Mission

A lot of people die every year because of their health not being tracked.
In Mathare, a busy neighborhood in Nairobi, many people struggle with health issues because their health isn’t tracked properly.
Even those who are educated often find it hard to get regular check-ups due to crowded clinics and high costs.
This means that conditions like diabetes and high blood pressure can go unnoticed until they become serious.
